Substat Optimization

Optimizes substat allocation for maximum damage on the first skill in the skills list. Does not currently support haste; Mastery only supported for Smite. Some stats like luck might be undervalued here if lucky hits help your rotation.
Click optimize to begin searching best allocation for the first skill.

Import Stats from ZDPS

  1. Open ZDPS and click on your character.
  2. In game, swap loadout to another different class/heavily different loadout. (remember to save current loadout if needed)
  3. Swap Back to your normal loadout.
  4. Un-equip and re-equip weapon.
  5. Wait 20-30 seconds to lose all temporary buffs like life wave.
  6. Go to the attributes tab in the entity inspector, and right click copy all attributes.
  7. Paste attributes in the box below and load.
